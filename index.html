Frequently asked questions

How long are your contracts?

We work on flexible terms agreed around your needs — typically an initial 12-month agreement with a straightforward notice period. We'd rather keep your business by performing than by locking you in.

What types of buildings and sectors do you cover?

We work with organisations of all sizes across the UK — offices, commercial and mixed-use buildings, and multi-site portfolios. If you're not sure whether your building fits, ask in the form — the review is free either way.

How does pricing work?

After your free facilities review, we quote a fixed monthly management fee based on your buildings and scope. Contractor and works costs are passed through transparently — you see every invoice, with no hidden mark-ups.

How fast is onboarding?

Most clients are fully onboarded within 4–6 weeks of the review. We take over supplier relationships and compliance records in stages so nothing gets dropped during handover.

Do you replace our in-house facilities manager, or support them?

Either. Many clients keep an in-house FM who uses us as their delivery arm — we handle the chasing, compliance and contractors so they can focus on strategy. Others outsource the function entirely. The review will show you which model fits.
